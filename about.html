About Us

Our mission at TrialStarter is to make more clinical trials possible. With 80% of drug trials failing due to a shortage of participants, we are missing opportunities to prevent, detect, and eradicate illness. TrialStarter can help by directly connecting drug companies, researchers, and potential patients in one consolidated website.
